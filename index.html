Vacation Site

Everyone loves going to a tropical island or a relaxing spa in the artic, but the place to be is actually at your local Taco Bell.

Taco Bell

Ah yes Taco Bell where you can easily spend twenty bucks on some tacos. It's the place to be and is open 24/7.

You can buy...

  • A quesidilla
  • A burrito
  • A taco

The hot sauces are pretty fuego. They include...

  • Mild
  • Medium
  • Diablo

Locations

If you are really adament about going far far away and relax in a nice vacation, theres probably a taco bell near there. Thier locations include...

  • Maui
  • Mexico
  • London
  • Australia
  • Kansas

So don't worry if you need a vacation away from your vacation, because Taco Bell has you covered.
